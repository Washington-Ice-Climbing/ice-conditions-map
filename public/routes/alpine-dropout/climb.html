Pitch 1: Climb approximately 60m of quality WI3.

Pitch 2: Walk up the slot canyon to the second pitch, which is another 60m of WI2-3.

Pitch 3: Drag the ropes to the crux pitch. This pitch is pretty steep and sustained and goes at WI3+.

Pitch 4: Continue another 500 ft up many more ice steps with WI2-3 sections up to 30m tall. The FA party chose to simul climb this section.

Pitch 5+: Continue up another 2000 ft of moderate to steep snow to the top of the ridge.
