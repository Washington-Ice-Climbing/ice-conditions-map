Most parties will carry 4-8 screws, a picket or two, and a 30 or 60 meter rope. Pickets can be helpful for a snow anchor above the ice crux. Crevasse rescue gear is also a must.

Because there is just a single technical pitch, some people chose to bring one mountaineering axe and one technical tool, but most will be more comfortable with two tools.

Before mid June or so, skis will greatly expedite the descent. Past that time, they might be more trouble than they are worth, but this of course depends on individual preference.
